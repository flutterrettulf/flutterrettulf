just_audio : An audio player plugin for Android, iOS, macOS, web, Linux and Windows.

Posted On Fri, Oct 21, 2022, Updated On Tue, Jan 31, 2023

A Flutter plugin for playing audio from a variety of sources, including remote URLs, local files, assets, and byte buffers. It also supports looping, seeking, volume control, playback speed control, and ducking.
